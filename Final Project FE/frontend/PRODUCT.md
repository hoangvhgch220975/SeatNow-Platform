# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Hungry diners and food enthusiasts looking for a reliable, fast, and delightful way to discover restaurants and book tables. They value ease of use, clear information (availability, menu, reviews), and a premium feel that matches their dining expectations.

## Product Purpose
SeatNow is a comprehensive restaurant discovery and booking platform. It aims to bridge the gap between diners and restaurants by providing real-time availability, personalized recommendations, and a frictionless booking experience. Success means a user finding a perfect table and completing a booking in under 2 minutes.

## Brand Personality
- **Sophisticated**: Refined and premium, like a fine dining experience.
- **Efficient**: No-nonsense, fast, and reliable.
- **Welcoming**: Warm, hospitable, and community-focused.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS "card-grid" dashboards.
- Overly cluttered food delivery apps with low-contrast labels.
- "Booking.com" style urgency tactics (e.g., "10 people are looking at this right now!").
- Harsh, un-tinted #000 or #fff interfaces.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over Clutter**: Focus on the restaurant's character and the booking action.
- **Hospitality-First**: The UI should feel like a digital maître d'—anticipating needs and guiding smoothly.
- **Sensory Experience**: Use high-quality imagery and subtle motion to evoke the feeling of dining.
- **Precision**: Every detail—alignment, spacing, typography—must be impeccable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Targeting WCAG 2.1 AA. Ensuring high contrast for critical booking paths and readable typography across all device sizes.
